Key Differences

Manatal charges per seat - $15-55 per user per month. A 5-person team pays $75-275/month.

Sharp Recruit uses flat-rate pricing. $29/mo Solo, $79/mo Elite. Your whole team. No per-seat math.

GitHub-Native Technical Recruiting

If you're hiring developers, you need to see their code - not just their resume. Manatal doesn't integrate with GitHub.

Sharp Recruit analyzes GitHub profiles: contribution patterns, code quality, tech stack, and activity levels.

Interview Intelligence

Manatal focuses on the ATS side - tracking candidates through your pipeline. Interview evaluation is basic.

Sharp Recruit includes advanced interview tools, AI-generated scorecards, and recruiter coaching features.

Job Board Integrations

Manatal connects to 2,500+ job boards and has strong LinkedIn integration for sourcing.

Sharp Recruit focuses on workflow tools rather than job board distribution.
