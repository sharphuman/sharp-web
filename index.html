Product Facts: Sharp Recruit

Product Name: Sharp Recruit

What it does: AI hiring intelligence layer for interview analysis, CV screening, and JD generation. Provides evidence-based scoring across six pillars with Hire If/Pass If decision frameworks.

Flagship Feature: Interview Analyzer - six-pillar analysis with direct evidence quotes, red/yellow flags, and follow-up questions.

Primary ICP: Recruiters and hiring managers at agencies (1-50 people) and internal teams (20-1000 employees). Any industry.

Pricing: Free ($0, 25 CV screens, 5 interviews/month), Solo ($25/month, 250 CV screens, 25 interviews), Elite ($250/month, 2500 CV screens, 250 interviews, 5 users).

Key Differentiator: Evidence-based analysis tied to specific moments in transcripts, not generic AI summaries.

Not an ATS: Works alongside existing tools as an intelligence layer.
