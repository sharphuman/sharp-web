Frequently asked questions

Is the free plan really free forever?

Yes. 25 CV screens, 5 interview analyses, unlimited JDs — every month, forever. No credit card required. No trial period.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account settings in one click. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

What happens if I hit my limits?

We'll let you know when you're approaching limits. You can upgrade anytime, or wait until next month when limits reset. We're not trying to trap you.

Why $25 and $250?

Because it's simple, memorable, and honest. $25/month is less than most people spend on coffee. $250/month for a team of 5 is still way cheaper than enterprise tools. We want you to feel good about paying, not resentful.

Do you offer annual billing?

Coming soon. When we add it, you'll get 2 months free (pay for 10, get 12).

What about bespoke/enterprise?

For teams who want custom-tuned AI analysis based on their hiring standards, we offer bespoke implementation. Book a call to discuss.

Can I export reports to share with clients?

Yes. All analyses can be exported to PDF and DOCX formats, ready to share with hiring managers or clients.

Do I need to replace my ATS?

No. Sharp Recruit is an intelligence layer, not an ATS replacement. It works alongside your existing tools — you keep your ATS, CRM, or spreadsheets.
