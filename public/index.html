What results can I expect?

Sharp Recruit users typically cut recruiting admin time by 50%+. Our outreach clients see 10-30 qualified meetings per month. META ad clients see 3-5x ROAS within the first quarter.

How quickly can we start?

Sharp Recruit access is immediate. Outreach and ad campaigns typically launch within 2 weeks. Custom AI projects vary but usually take 2-6 weeks.

Do I need technical skills?

Nope. Sharp Recruit is designed to be as simple as typing a prompt. For our services, we handle all the technical work.

What's the pricing?

Sharp Recruit has a free tier, then $29/mo (Solo) or $79/mo (Elite). Outreach and META services are monthly retainers plus ad spend. Bespoke AI projects are fixed-price based on scope.

What if it doesn't work?

We're upfront if we don't think we can help. Most of our services are month-to-month, so if you're not seeing value, you can walk away.

Who do you typically work with?

Mostly B2B companies ‚Äî startups, agencies, recruiting firms, and SMBs looking to scale. Our sweet spot is businesses that have product-market fit but need help with lead gen, hiring, or operational efficiency.
